How We Sustain the Community

Building a sustainable ecosystem for Rwanda's developer community through commercial work that funds open source and community initiatives

Our Model

DevRW is built on a sustainable model where commercial software development projects fund our open source work and community initiatives. This approach allows us to:

  • Maintain and improve open source tools like the Rwanda Locations Library
  • Support community events, meetups, and learning resources
  • Provide a platform for developers to connect with job opportunities
  • Invest in social impact projects that serve Rwanda's development needs

Custom Development

We build custom software solutions for businesses and organizations, applying our expertise to solve real-world problems.

Developer Placement

We connect skilled developers from our community with companies seeking talent, creating opportunities for both sides.

Social Impact

Revenue from commercial work funds social impact projects like Itegure, serving Rwanda's education and development needs.

Work With Us

When you hire DevRW for your project or work with us to find developer talent, you're directly supporting Rwanda's open source ecosystem and developer community.
