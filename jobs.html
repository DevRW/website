Live openings live elsewhere.

We don't run a job board on this site. Active openings are tracked in a public Google Sheet that the community keeps up to date. Below: how the routing works, and how to get into the pool.

View live openings (Google Sheet)
01 / how_it_works

No board on this site. A pipeline.

Companies send us role briefs. We circulate them in WhatsApp, match candidates, and forward shortlists. The current openings live on a public Google Sheet — easier to keep accurate than a hand-coded page.

01.1

You submit a CV

A real CV, a working portfolio link, and a couple of lines on what you're after. We confirm receipt and add you to the directory.

01.2

A role comes in

A hiring partner sends us a brief. We add it to the public Google Sheet, circulate it in #jobs on WhatsApp, and reach out directly to people who match.

01.3

You decide

If interested, we forward your CV. Interview, negotiate, sign — all between you and the company. We don't take a cut from your salary.

02 / openings_sheet

Where the actual listings live.

We use a single Google Sheet as the source of truth for active openings. It's public and view-only. Filter, sort, copy a row — whatever's useful. We update it whenever a new role comes in or an old one closes.
